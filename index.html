About Me

Welcome to my research profile! I am an Assistant Professor of Library and Information Studies in the Department of STEM and Professional Studies at Old Dominion University. My research focuses on knowledge organization, metadata, ontologies, health informatics, and AI ethics.

I am particularly interested in how knowledge organization systems can enhance healthcare data integration, support transparent AI systems, and advance semantic interoperability. My work bridges technical implementation with considerations of epistemic justice and digital accountability.
